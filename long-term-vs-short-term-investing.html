Long Term vs Short Term Investing

Investing in the stock market can be done in different ways. The two most common approaches are long-term investing and short-term investing. Both have their own advantages and risks.

What is Long-Term Investing?

Long-term investing means buying stocks and holding them for several years. Investors focus on company growth, fundamentals, and compounding benefits.
